Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide online courses to help families navigate pornography issues in the home. There will be courses for parents in how to help their children, including how to talk about sexuality in healthy, shame-free ways. There will also be courses for newlywed couples where one or both partners has had a journey with pornography or is still on a journey with this habit. This course will also teach healthy sexuality within the relationship and helpful ways to apporoach this topic as a couple.

Audience

The audience of this website are adult members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints who are looking for helpful ways to navigate pornography issues in their families. This is not a website to help those who struggle with pornography overcome their habit. It is a website for those supporting someone they love who is struggling with an unwanted pornoography habit. The audience will also consist of parents who want to be better informed to help their children navigate our highly sexualized world and hopefully avoid developing a pornography habit.

Content

Home page

Good Intimacy is for individuals who are looking for information on how to form or further develop a healthy sexual relationship. There are a lot of ways our sexuality and/or our sexual relationships can get off course and not be everything we hope. Low-sex marriages, unsatisfying sex, pornography habits, infidelity, and other sex-related frustrations are all symptoms of not having a highly developed sexual self. The courses on this website will teach the areas of personal and emotional development that are also sexual development.

[Courses]

Visitors will come to this website to learn about the courses offered as well as register and pay for courses. They will also come to this website for general information about discipline of sexual development. They will also come wanting to know the credentials of the teachers of the courses.

The website will need to contain courses, information about the courses, general information about sexual development, and a way to accept payment for courses. There will need to be an “about me” section for my bio information as for now, I am the only teacher. Hopefully in the future, as more classes are offered, this will change to an “about us” section.

[Sexual Development]

For the first offering, this site will mostly be a place for members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. There aren’t a lot of good resources that members of The Church feel comfortable turning to in order to learn more about healthy sexuality. The online Church manual of Bishop Counseling Resources has a section on how to help individuals who are trying to overcome a pornography habit. It states, “Encourage the member to seek information about healthy sexuality, proper sexual expression, and the role of a sex drive within the boundaries God has established.”

This statement demonstrates that one current recommendation a bishop should tell an individual who is trying to overcome a pornography habit is to learn more about healthy sexuality. The only problem is, there aren’t good church-sponsored resources for an individual who wants to learn more about healthy sexuality to turn to. This website will need to not only offer helpful courses but reassure the customers that the principles that will be taught are also in line with the teachings and doctrine of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.

There may need to be free sample videos of me teaching different concepts so they feel comfortable with me as their teacher. There will also need to be a hopeful vibe. Having difficulty with your sexuality is incredibly frustrating and even painful. Many tend to just give up hope and assume this will be a trial for them until they day they die. The website will need to demonstrate that growth, development, improvement, and overcoming are all possible. They won’t happen quickly, development always takes time, but they can happen.
