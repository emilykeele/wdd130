Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website it to promote the adventous and family-friendly atmosphere of Wild Dog Rafting Company. We will show images of adults and children laughing and smiling while rafting. We will show images of our well-trained rafting guides. We will show people wearing their safety gear like life jackets and helmets while rafting on large rapids. This will benefit our company by building trust in our customers before they visit us and giving them information about our various trip options. This website will increase awareness of our company, demonstrate we are a great option for rafting adventures in Cache Valley, and increase our revenue due to increased bookings after our audience visits the website.

Audience

The Wild Dog Rafting Company audience is families and individuals who are living in or visiting Cache Valley Utah and interesting in taking a rafting adventure. The site will need to appeal to families, college students, and mature singles as Cache Valley is a hub for several diverse populations. Something all of our audience will share is a desire for an adventure. The site will have a fun and playful vibe through fonts and colors while also being clean, orderly, and professional.
